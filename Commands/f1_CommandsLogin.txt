#Commands available at login stage

HELP -->Show all available commands for Login
HELPc command -->Show use and useCase of command
CLEAR -->Clear the terminal

BUYER>name balance -->Connect as a buyer called name(first character has to be a letter) and owning balance(has to be a floating positive number)
SELLER>name -->Connect as a seller called name(first character has to be a letter)
AUCTIONEER>pinCode -->Connect as the auctioneer with the pinCode(a 4 digits natural number)
